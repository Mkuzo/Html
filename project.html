Steps to Make a Sandwich

Follow these simple steps to assemble a tasty sandwich:

  1. Get two slices of bread.
  2. Spread butter or mayo on one or both slices.
  3. Add your preferred fillings (cheese, deli meat, veggies).
  4. Season with salt, pepper, or condiments.
  5. Close the sandwich, cut if desired, and serve.
